About Us

Haatify is a digital initiative aimed at transforming the traditional haat culture into a modern, tech-enabled experience. Rooted in the vibrant spirit of local marketplaces, Haatify provides a smart, accessible, and organized platform for vendors and buyers alike.

We believe that the charm of haat bazaars shouldn't be lost with time — instead, it should evolve. That's why Haatify blends tradition with innovation, offering digital space for local sellers to showcase their products, manage inventory, and connect with a wider audience. Buyers get the ease of browsing diverse items — from fresh produce to handmade crafts — all at their fingertips.
